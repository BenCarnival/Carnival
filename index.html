Hello. Congratulations. If you are reading this it means you have been invited to Blatt's First Annual Carnival Party.

Don't miss out on this chance to win fabulous prizes*. Blatt's Carnival Party will consist of seven rounds of competition. The winner of each round will move on to the final round and a chance to win the grand prize! Food and refreshments will be provided but feel free to bring more. Read below for complete schedule of events. If you are interested in running an activity or showcasing an arts & crafts exhibit please contact The Ringmaster. The pool will be open for use (except during Duck Grab) so bring a suit.


Schedule of Events


0. Competitor Meet and Greet


Mingle among your future friends and enemies. Everyone is a winner this round (which means no one wins).

1. Ladderball Toss



Solo ladderball playoff competition. Winner will advance to final around. Playoff format TBD.

2. Raffle Ticket Draw



One number will be pulled from a bucket and winner will advance to the final round. Tickets are awarded for the following action:
Showing Up - 3 tickets
Showing Up Before 1:30 - 1 extra ticket
Bringing a Friend - 2 tickets
Bringing a Food or Drink Item - 2 tickets
RSVPing ahead of time - 1 ticket
Random Act of Kindness (as determined by the Ringmaster) - 1 ticket

3. Blatt's Blasters


Come play The Ringmaster's very own DIY bespoke arcade game Blatt's Blasters. Winner will advance to final round.

4. Guess Sam Richman's Weight


How much does Sam Richman weigh? We will put him on a scale and everyone guesses just how many pounds he weighs (to the nearest tenth of a pound). Closest guess will advance to the next round.

5. Popcorn Eating Contest


Solo popcorn eating contest. Fastest to eat a ton of popcorn is champion. Winner will advance to final around. Playoff format TBD.

6. Duck Grab


150 small rubber ducks will be thrown into the pool. Only a small handful will have a winner mark underneath. Race to find your golden ticket to the final around.

7. Final Raffle Ticket Draw


One number will be pulled from a bucket and winner will advance to the final round. Tickets are kept from round two plus the following action:
Participating in previous rounds of competition - 1 ticket per round



FINAL ROUND


The final competition is a surprise
